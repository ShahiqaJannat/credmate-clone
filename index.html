About

Credmate

Credmate, established in 2020, is a cutting-edge, fast, secure, and an ahead-of-its-time application that allows you to become a smarter manager of your credit buying selling ,borrowing & lending requirements . Its simple and straight forward User Interface (UI) lets you to check who owes or to whom you owe funds with a single tap. Moreover, it facilitates secure decision making on whether a person deserves to be lent money or not. You can always find this out from his/her Credmate Score or Transaction History. Therefore, Credmate is an Application which is a lot more than a fund borrowing and lending platform. Credmate is the first platform which inculcates Financial Discipline among its users unlike any other application of the same nature. Credmate is a Software as a Service (SaaS) established by an visionary , service-oriented, and world-class team. With its vision to build a common platform to manage credit buying selling borrowing & lending thus reducing risk of bad debts in the world, Credmate is nothing short of a revolution in the Financial Technology sector. Team Credmate is confident that Credmate’s contribution to the Fintech sector will be remarkable. Team Credmate is devoted to serving generations of upcoming Micro Small medium & large Businesses, Students, and in fact every user who has a transaction on credit basis. With Credmate’s arrival, one can confidently claim that the Future indeed is here. Make Credmate your best mate!
